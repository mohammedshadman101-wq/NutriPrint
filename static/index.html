They are busy with daily livelihood struggles and rarely search online for nutrition guidelines. NutriPrint solves this elegantly. By routing customized dietary printouts through school teachers, the advice is physically printed and reaches the kitchen wall. Every single morning, parents see a structured, budget-friendly meal suggestion using locally available grains like Ragi and Jowar. Awareness is achieved passively, requiring zero technology adoption from the parents.
