Project NutriPrint

Problem Statement: Lack of awareness and training among parents regarding healthy eating habits for children.

Parents in rural and semi-urban Karnataka lack systematic, easily accessible awareness about balanced child nutrition. They are busy with daily livelihood struggles and rarely search online for nutrition guidelines.

NutriPrint solves this elegantly. By routing customized dietary printouts through school teachers, the advice is physically printed and reaches the kitchen wall. Every single morning, parents see a structured, budget-friendly meal suggestion using locally available grains like Ragi and Jowar. Awareness is achieved passively, requiring zero technology adoption from the parents.
